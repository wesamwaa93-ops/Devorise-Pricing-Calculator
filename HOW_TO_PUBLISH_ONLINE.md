# How to Put Your Devorise Pricing Calculator Online (Free & Easy)

Since this is a simple HTML/CSS/JS application, you can host it online for free in less than **60 seconds**. Once uploaded, your sales team can access it from any browser or phone using a custom link!

Here are the two easiest ways:

## Option 1: Netlify Drop (Fastest - No account required)
1. Open this folder on your computer: `C:\Users\wesam\OneDrive\Desktop\ss\Devorise_Pricing_Calculator`
2. Go to **[app.netlify.com/drop](https://app.netlify.com/drop)** in your web browser.
3. Drag and drop the entire `Devorise_Pricing_Calculator` folder from your Desktop into the circle on the Netlify webpage.
4. Netlify will instantly generate a live, public link (e.g., *https://devorise-pricing-app.netlify.app*).
5. Share this link with your sales reps!

## Option 2: GitHub Pages (Best for long-term updates)
1. If you use GitHub, create a new repository called `devorise-pricing-calculator`.
2. Upload the `index.html`, `style.css`, and `app.js` files into the repository.
3. Go to the repository **Settings** -> **Pages**.
4. Under "Build and deployment", set the Source to "Deploy from a branch" and select `main`.
5. Click **Save**. Within 1-2 minutes, GitHub will give you a live URL.

---
**💡 Tip for Sales Reps:**
Once the link is live, tell your team to bookmark it or add it to their phone's home screen for instant access during discovery calls.
